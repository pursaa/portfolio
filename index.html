All about me

I went to classes for electronics and was in that field for a while but my bosses always pushed me to be the leader of teams so I ended up being was a supervisor most of the time. I spent time troubleshooting to component level and build/fixing computers. I love to learn and seem to always find myself going to school or surffing the internet for information. I always liked to make videos and editing photos. I wanted to learn to create websites but I never had the time. I finally had time and started taking graphic design classes three years ago. I only have two courses left for my AA. I guit my 40 hour a week job in 2016 so I could focus more time on my classes and got my licence as a care giver. Now I work part time as a care giver helping those that need help and going to classes the reast of the time. I felt I needed more programming experiance so I signed up at Epicodus to really learn coding.
